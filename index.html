Axolotl's

The Axolotl (pronounced ax-oh-lot-ul) is a Mexican Walking Fish. Axolotl is not actually a fish but an amphibian, a salamander.

Red Panda

The Red Panda is an endangered species. It is also called a lesser panda and is a red bear-cat.

Ferret

Ferrets are friendly, intelligent and really active. Compared to other small mammals they are high maintenance in food because they need lots of protein and they digest food really fast. So ferrets are not ideal for owners who are mostly away.

Cats

Cats are very common household pets and pets in general. There is a whole variety of types of cats from looks to how they act.

Songbirds

Songbirds are exactly what they are called. But they dont literally sing but they make sweet-sounding to demanding noises.
